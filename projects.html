Captain Alex

2026

Built during Thomas More Professional Week, Captain Alex is an IoT powered product meant to help farmers in East Africa against the many issues they face, like pests, unpredictable weather and soil conditions. In our team, I worked on the regression model that factored in soil conditions in East Africa, live weather data from OpenMeteo to give farmers a daily report on their farm via SMS messages, alongside dynamic advice on how to maximize their yield based on the state of the farm and weather.

Scout Agent Search

Ongoing

Scout is an open-source project I started after making my first OpenClaw agent. Initially I faced some difficulties pulling data from web sources, and after looking into it I realized that the way agents pull data from the web is optimized for human readability, but not for AI Consumption. As a result, I started building Scout, a "search engine" for OpenClaw models to pull data in recognizable JSON formats and produce more accurate and efficient results for users. Check back later for more progress!

NumPy Pydantic OpenClaw FastAPI
